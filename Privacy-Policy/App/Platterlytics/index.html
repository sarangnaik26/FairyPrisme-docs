Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 1, 2026

Introduction

Platterlytics ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your business and personal privacy. Our application is built with an offline-first architecture, meaning your data stays where it belongs: on your device.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the Platterlytics application.

Data Collection and Storage

We do not collect, transmit, or store your business data on any external servers.

All data you manage within the application is stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Menu Items: Names, prices, and categories.
  • Transaction History: Bills, dates, and amounts.
  • Business Details: Information entered in settings for bill generation (e.g., business name, address).
  • Analytics: All performance metrics are calculated locally from your stored transactions.

Since this data is stored locally, we have no access to it. If you uninstall the application or clear its data via system settings, all information will be permanently deleted.

App Permissions

Platterlytics may request the following permissions to provide its services:

  • Internet Access: Required by the underlying web-view framework to render the interface. We do not use this access to upload your personal or business data.
  • Storage: Required to save your menu and billing data locally on the device.

Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party analytics (like Google Analytics), advertising SDKs, or tracking scripts. Your business patterns and sales data remain private and are never shared with third parties.

Data Security

Because your data is stored locally, its security depends on the security of your device. We recommend using device-level security measures (like passcodes or biometrics) to protect your information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as we add new features. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
