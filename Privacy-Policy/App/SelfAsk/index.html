Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

Introduction

SelfAsk ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This application is built with a "Privacy First" philosophy. We believe your thoughts are yours alone.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

Data Collection and Storage

We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any external server.

All data you enter into the application (including questions, thoughts, and settings) is stored locally on your device using your device's internal storage mechanisms (such as LocalStorage or IndexedDB).

If you choose to uninstall the application or clear its data via your device settings, this locally stored data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered by us.

App Permissions

The application may request the following permissions:

  • Internet Access (android.permission.INTERNET): This is a standard permission required by the underlying framework (Capacitor/WebView) to render the application interface. We do not use this permission to transmit your personal data to any external servers.

Third-Party Services

This application does not use any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs. We do not share your data with third parties because we do not have access to your data.

Data Security

Since your data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's security.

If you use the optional "App Lock" feature, your password is cryptographically hashed (SHA-256) and stored locally. Please note that this feature is a deterrent and does not encrypt the underlying database files on a rooted device.

Children's Privacy

Our application does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
