Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ArcPoint ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information. ArcPoint is developed with privacy as a priority.

1. Information Collection and Use

ArcPoint is designed to be a local-first application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers. All information you enter into the App, including novel data, reading progress, and settings, is stored locally on your device.

2. Permissions

To provide its features, the App may request the following permissions:

  • Internet: Used only for features you explicitly trigger, such as opening external web links to novels.
  • Storage/Media: Used to allow you to select and display images (covers) for your novels.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., novel hosting sites). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites.

4. Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device, your data remains on your device until you choose to delete the App or its data manually.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at fairyprisme@gmail.com.
