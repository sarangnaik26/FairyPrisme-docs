Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 24, 2026

We respect your privacy. "Stone Paper Scissor Reflex" is a standalone, family-friendly game. It does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. What happens on your device stays on your device.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

When you download and play "Stone Paper Scissor Reflex", we do not require you to provide any personal information. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, or any other personally identifiable information (PII).

2. How the Game Works (Local Data Only)

Your game progress, including your high score, earned coins, and unlocked skins, is saved entirely locally on your device. This data is never uploaded to external servers or accessible to us. If you uninstall the game, this data will be deleted.

3. Device Sensor Usage

The game utilizes your device's accelerometer sensor to control the horizontal movement of the player object via tilting. This sensor data is processed locally in real-time solely for gameplay mechanics. It is never recorded, stored, or transmitted over the internet.

4. Required Permissions

The application requires the following Android permission:

  • INTERNET: This permission is required by the underlying framework (specifically for rendering optimal fonts) when the game starts. The game does not use this permission to track you, show remote ads, or upload any user data.

5. Children's Privacy

Our game is built for everyone, including children. In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar global regulations, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Because our app does not collect any personal data from any user, children are inherently protected.

6. Third-Party Services

The game currently does not integrate any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising networks. There are no embedded trackers in the application.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.
