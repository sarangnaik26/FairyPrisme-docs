FLOATY BIRD REVERSE

Welcome, Float-master! Ready to defy gravity? Here's the lowdown on how to keep our blue buddy in the air (or on the ceiling!).

1. The Basics: Tap to Flap!

It's simpler than calculating the trajectory of a rogue coconut. Just TAP anywhere on your screen to make the bird flap its wings and stay aloft!

2. Master the Modes

Normal Mode: Classic flappy action. Gravity pulls you down; you tap to go up. Keep it steady!
Reverse Mode: The floor is lava... but also the sky? In this mode, gravity is inverted! You fall upwards, and tapping pushes you downwards. Brain-bending fun!
Switch Gravity: Every time you pass a pipe, gravity flips! Up is down, down is up... good luck keeping track!

3. Watch Out for the Wood!

Those wooden pipes are sturdy. One bonk and it's game over. Navigate through the gaps to rack up points!

4. High Scores

Your best efforts are saved automatically. Can you beat your own record in every mode?
