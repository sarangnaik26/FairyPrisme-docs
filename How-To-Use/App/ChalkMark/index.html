Getting Started

Before you can track your progress, ChalkMark needs to know your schedule and term dates.

1
Set Academic Term: Go to Settings and set your semester's start and end dates.
2
Setup Timetable: Head to the Timetable tab and add your subjects. Make sure to mark holidays!
📅

Tracking Attendance

Once setup is complete, you'll see your attendance cards on the home screen.

✔
Marking: Simply tap "Present", "Absent", or "Canceled" when prompted or in the daily view.
📊
Analytics: Tap the "Overall Attendance" card to see your history, set goals, and see how much you can afford to skip.
💡 Pro tip: Use "Canceled" for when the college cancels a class. It won't hurt your percentage!
🔮

Using the Simulator

The Simulator is your crystal ball. It helps you see the future of your attendance.

1
Open Simulator: Look for the "Simulator" button in your attendance detail views.
2
Mark Future Days: Tap dates on the calendar to see how attending or skipping them affects your final percentage.
3
Term Projection: Turn this ON to automatically assume you'll attend all remaining classes in the semester.
